[too-MUHL-choo-uh s, tyoo-]
  1. adj. full of tumult or riotousness; marked by disturbance and uproar
  2. adj. raising a great clatter and commotion; disorderly or noisy
  3. adj. highly agitated, as the mind or emotions; distraught; turbulent
